Sagacious Mermaid ITIS 3135 Web Evaluations

Bad Website

  • You want to stay away from a website that forces you to auto-download files
  • Contrast: Content on top of content, poor-quality animations, hard-to-find navigation
  • Repition: There is no consistency with alignment, there are random highlights on the text.
  • Alignment: There is no oragnization to the content which makes it hard to read and keep up with.
  • Proximity:This website doesn't follow any proximity, everything on this website is very random and not related to each other.

Good Website

  • YouTube uses contrast effectively in its design
  • Repetition: YouTube maintains consistency throughout its platform by repeating certain design elements.
  • Alignment: YouTube generally follows alignment principles to ensure that elements on the page are visually organized and coherent
  • Proximity: YouTube uses proximity to group related elements together and create visual hierarchy
