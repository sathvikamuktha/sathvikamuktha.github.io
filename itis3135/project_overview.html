Client Project Overview

Project Overview:

  • The application will be a website for the Tamil Student Organization (TSO) at UNC Charlotte. The primary purpose of this website is to serve as a comprehensive online platform for promoting and celebrating Tamil language, culture, and heritage. It aims to foster a vibrant community by providing information about TSO's activities, events, and initiatives, enabling members and interested individuals to engage with Tamil traditions and customs.
  • The website will serve as a central hub for current TSO members to stay informed about upcoming events, activities, news, and announcements related to the organization. The website will also be accessible to the general students of the UNC Charlotte community who are interested in Tamil culture, arts, literature, and traditions. It will serve as a gateway for them to learn about and appreciate the diverse cultural offerings of the Tamil Student Organization.
  • The website will provide a comprehensive introduction to the Tamil Student Organization, detailing its vision, E-board team, about the club, and recent event. It will feature a robust events and activities section, showcasing upcoming cultural events, traditional celebrations, literature discussions, language classes, movie nights, and karaoke sessions.
