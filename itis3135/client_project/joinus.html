How to join TSO?

  • Membership is free of cost for all UNCC student with a valid Student-ID number

  • To sign up for membership click here and complete the form.

Membership Eligibility:

  • The TSO is open to all students currently enrolled at UNC Charlotte, regardless of their ethnic background or Tamil language proficiency. Non-student members, such as alumni, faculty, staff, and community members, are also welcome to join the organization.

Membership Benefits:

  • Opportunity to engage with and learn about Tamil culture, language, and traditions.
  • Access to all TSO events, activities, and initiatives at discounted rates or free of cost.
  • Leadership and volunteering experiences through involvement in TSO's executive team & committees.
